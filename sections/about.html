About me

Early days

Since early days I've always been proactive: I bought my first electric guitar selling rings!

So when I turn 16 I started my first real job: McDonald'

After two years of hard work, be able to go on holidays with my friends and help economically at home I decided to look new challenges while I was studying Audiovisual Arts. So I worked as a waitress at Sheraton Hotel Buenos Aires and after 6 months I became the manager of a vegan restorant at Palermo's neightburhood

But I had another plans for my life: I wanted to move to another state. That's where I question myself: how can I make money when I move? That's where "Melito's: vegan delicatessen" was born

My own business

In September of 2015 I started working on my new business: what would be the proposal? what should I sale? How I was going to sell? I had some money saved and I spend almost everything in things for my new business

On January of 2016 I moved to another state: Paraná City where my aunt Rosa and grannys Sara and Julio lived

I am really thankfull for they letting me go and live with them. They helped me a lot with my first days of Melito's ♥

I learned how to be a real entrepeneur and a business woman for 6 years with Melito's. In the middle, I made my studies as a cook and restaruant administrations and other professional formations like digital marketing, business, food photography and more.

IT world

After 6 years of hard working as a cook I realised I wanted to accomplish another wishes I had since I was little: work with my computer.

So on Septembre 2021 I looked what can I do, what did I like about IT World. That's when I decided to try out UX UI Design.

And jackspot! This career is Everything I love: being curious, creative, proactive, help people to make their own web products.
